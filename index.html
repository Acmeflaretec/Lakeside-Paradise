About the project

Lakeside Paradise

Ajmera Lakeside Paradise, a premium residential development by Ajmera Group, is located in Yelahanka, Bengaluru. This exquisite project offers a grand clubhouse with panoramic views of the serene lake and the city skyline. Designed to provide a lifestyle of luxury and comfort, Ajmera Lakeside Paradise boasts finest amenities, including a spa, health club, salon, guest rooms, and a swimming pool. The spacious homes feature contemporary designs with floor-to-ceiling windows, flooding interiors with natural light while offering breathtaking lake views. Surrounded by lush green landscapes and parks, it offers an ideal living environment for nature enthusiasts. Ajmera Lakeside Paradise is the perfect choice for those seeking a luxurious and tranquil living experience.
