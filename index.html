ABOUT THE PROJECT

Ajmera Marina

Ajmera Marina, a premium residential development by Ajmera Group, is located in Yelahanka, Bengaluru. This exquisite project offers a grand clubhouse with panoramic views of the serene lake and the city skyline. Designed to provide a lifestyle of luxury and comfort, Ajmera Marina boasts finest amenities, including a spa, health club, salon, guest rooms, and a swimming pool. The spacious homes feature contemporary designs with floor-to-ceiling windows, flooding interiors with natural light while offering breathtaking lake views. Surrounded by lush green landscapes and parks, it offers an ideal living environment for nature enthusiasts.
